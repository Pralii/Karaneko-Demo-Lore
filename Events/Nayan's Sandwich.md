Your first quest is to make a sandwich for your hungry brother before he tries to beat you in Touhou.

"Nayan's Special" is a complex recipe made of: sausage(two layers for extra happiness), cheddar cheese, bell pepper, tomato, avocado, corn (don't forget!), hot sauce, egg, olives, butter, mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard. Thankfully, Karam is a master chef.

- Tease him by going in his room with the ingredients.
- Tease his nostrils by eating the sandwich before entering.
